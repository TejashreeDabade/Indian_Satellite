Cartosat-1

CARTOSAT-1 is the first Indian Remote Sensing Satellite capable of providing in-orbit stereo images. The images were used for Cartographic applications meeting the global requirements.

 

Launch date 5 May 2005
Launch site SHAR Centre Sriharikota India
Launch vehicle PSLV- C6
Orbit 618 km Polar Sun Synchronous
Payloads PAN FORE, PAN - AFT
Orbit Period 97 min
Number of Orbits Per day 14

Type of Satellite: Earth Observation
Manufacturer: ISRO
Owner: ISRO
Application: Earth Observation
